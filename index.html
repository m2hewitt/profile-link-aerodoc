Professional Profile

As a seasoned Global Trade and Logistics Specialist with over 18 years of experience, I excel at navigating the intricate landscape of international commerce. My expertise lies in demystifying complex supply chains and enabling businesses to expand their global footprint with confidence and clarity. I am passionate about creating strategic, end-to-end logistics solutions that not only ensure compliance but also drive significant operational efficiencies. By acting as a dedicated partner to my clients, I transform regulatory hurdles into competitive advantages, ensuring that high-value technology goods move seamlessly across borders. My work is centered on the principle that logistics should be a silent, powerful engine for growth, not a source of friction.

My career has been dedicated to mastering the nuances of global distribution, particularly for the IT, telecommunications, and satellite industries. I have a proven track record of designing and executing comprehensive logistics strategies for companies aiming to penetrate new markets without establishing a legal entity. This is achieved by leveraging my deep expertise as an importer of record, a role in which I assume full responsibility for customs clearance, duty and tax management, and adherence to all international trade regulations. From managing DDP shipments to over 170 countries to coordinating last-mile delivery for sensitive data center hardware, I ensure that every shipment is handled with precision, integrity, and a proactive approach to problem-solving. I am not just a logistics provider; I am a strategic asset to my clients, empowering them to trade globally with the same ease as they do domestically.
